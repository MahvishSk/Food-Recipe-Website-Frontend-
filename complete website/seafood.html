Prawn Pepper Fry

Prawn pepper fry is a recipe which was on our mind for quite some time. It is spicy, hot and totally delicious. we made it comepletely south indian style, but there are so many version of this recipe. Will share all of them soon.
Hope you will give this a try and let us know how it turns out for you..
NUTRITIONS:- | Calories: 119kcal | Carbohydrates: 5g | Protein: 1g | Fat: 11g | Saturated Fat: 1g | | Polyunsaturated Fat: 3g | Monounsaturated Fat: 7g | Trans Fat: 0.04g | Cholesterol: 6mg | Sodium: 114mg | | Potassium: 68mg | Fiber: 1g | Sugar: 2g | Vitamin A: 158IU | Vitamin C: 5mg | Calcium: 12mg | Iron: 0.4mg

Prep time

10 min.

Cook time

30 min.

Serving

4 Serving

Instruction

Step 1

Mix all the ingredients for marination and mix well. Set aside. Heat oil in a pan, add in prawns and fry for a min. Now remove to a bowl.

Step 2

In the same oil and add in onions, chillies and curry leaves. Season with salt and cook for a min.

Step 3

Add in ginger garlic paste and saute for a min. Add in capsicum and saute for 2 mins.

Step 4

Now add in prawns and mix well.

Step 5

Add in some garam masala powder and toss well. Cover and cook for 4 to 5 mins.

Step 6

Now add in pepper and coriander leaves, Toss well. Serve.

Ingredients

* Prawns - 20 Chilli Powder - 1 tsp Turmeric Powder / Manjal Podi - 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste - 1 tsp Salt to taste Oil - 3 tbsp

* Onion - 1 sliced thinly Green Chilli - 1 or 2 slit Curry Leaves a sprig

* Bell Pepper / Capsicum - 1 sliced thinly Garam Masala Powder - 1.5 tsp Fresh Black Pepper Powder - 1 tsp

*Coriander Leaves a handful finely chopped

Tools

* Stove

* Frying Pan

* Measuring Spoons

* Measuring Cups

* Bowls/Spoons
