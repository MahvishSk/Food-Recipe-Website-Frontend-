Honey Garlic Chicken

Honey Garlic Chicken recipe just became a new family favourite. Golden, tender bites of chicken sticky, glossy with honey-garlic sauce. So good. No deep fry.
NUTRITION:- Calories: 447kcal, Carbohydrates: 27g, Protein: 20, Fat: 29g, Saturated Fat:6g, Polyunsaturated Fat:5g, Monounsaturated Fat: 15g, Trans Fat: 0.1g, Cholesterol: 112mg, Sodium: 860mg, Potassium:291mg, Fiber: 0.4g, Sugar: 18g, Vitamin A: 149IU,Vitamin C: 2mg, Calcium: 21mg, Iron: 1mg

Prep time

10min.

Cook time

20min.

Serving

3 serving

Instruction

Step 1

Split chicken breast in half so 2 breasts makes 4 pieces

Step 2

Dust chicken with flour (gives the chicken a slight crust so the sauce clings to it better!)

Step 3

Sear chicken, add butter, garlic, vinegar, soy sauce, honey.

Step 4

Simmer until the sauce thickens. Turn chicken to coat in the amazing honey garlic sauce.

Ingredients

* chicken thighs, bone in or out, with or without skin

* Salt and pepper,2 teaspoons garlic powder, to season 6 cloves garlic, crushed/p>

* 1/3 cup honey.

* 1/4 cup water (or chicken broth)

*2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar (or apple cider vinegar, or any white vinegar) 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Tools

* Stove

* Frying Pan

* Measuring Spoons

* Measuring Cups
