Spicy Coconut Noodles

This 20-minute Asian-inspired dinner traditionally uses rice noodles, but fettuccine is a delicious substitute.
NUTRITIONS:- Calories 480 Total Fat 25g 32% Saturated Fat 19g 95% Sodium 720mg 31% Total Carbohydrate 58g 21% Total Sugars 4g Protein 12g

Prep time

10 min.

Cook time

20 min.

Serving

3 Serving

Instruction

Step 1

Cook the noodles according to the package directions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2

Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, over medium-high heat, combine the coconut milk, tomato paste, chili powder, salt, and chili paste (if desired).

Step 3

Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 2 to 3 minutes.

Step 4

Stir the drained noodles into the sauce and toss. Divide among individual bowls. Top with the scallions, sprouts, basil, and coconut (if desired).

Ingredients

* 8 ounces rice noodles or fettuccine 1 13.5-ounce can unsweetened coconut milk 3 tablespoons tomato paste.

* 1 teaspoon chili powder 1 teaspoon kosher salt 1 tablespoon chili paste or sauce (optional).

* 3 scallions, thinly sliced 8 ounces bean sprouts.

*16 basil leaves, whole or torn ¼ cup shredded coconut, toasted (optional).

Tools

* Stove

* Frying Pan

* Measuring Spoons

* Measuring Cups

* Bowls/Spoons
