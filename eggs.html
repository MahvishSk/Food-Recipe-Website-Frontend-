Egg Masala

This Egg Masala is one of the most demanding recipes at home and am so glad that it is one of the popular recipes here in this space too
NUTRITIONS:- Calories 303 Calories from Fat 14% Daily Value *Fat 16g 25% Saturated Fat 3g 19% Trans Fat 0.1g Polyunsaturated Fat 4g Monounsaturated Fat 8g Cholesterol 246mg 82% Sodium 2057mg 89% Potassium 735 mg21% Carbohydrates 29g 10% Fiber 7g 29% Sugar 13g 14% Protein 13g 26% Vitamin A 1831IU 37% Vitamin C 289mg 350% Calcium 171mg 17% Iron 3mg17% * Percent Daily Values are based on a 2000 calorie diet.

Prep time

15 min.

Cook time

25 min.

Serving

2 Serving

Instruction

Step 1

Heat 1/2 teaspoon oil add 2 tablespoon coriander seeds, 4 red chillies, 1 teaspoon pepper, 1 teaspoon fennel seeds. Roast until golden. Then add 3 tablespoon coconut and roast until golden brown. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Step 2

Add 1/2 teaspoon oil add 20 small onion along with 2 tomatoes. Saute until raw smell leaves and tomatoes shrink. Transfer to the same bowl and grind it to a semi fine paste along with water, Set it aside.

Step 3

Rinse the pan then to the same pan add 2 tablespoon oil heat it. Add one 1 inch cinnamon, 1 clove, 1 cardamom, a small piece stone flower, 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds let it splutter then add 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds let it splutter. Add few curry leaves let it splutter then give a quick saute.

Step 4

Add 1 medium sized onion finely chopped along with 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste, salt to taste. Saute until onions turn golden.Add 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon red chilli powder and 1/4 teaspoon garam masala powder.Give a quick saute.

Step 5

Add the masala paste. Rinse mixer with 1 cup water and add it too. Mix well. Adjust consistency by adding little more water if needed. Let it boil.

Step 6

Cook covered for 5-7 mins. Meawhile we can roast the eggs. Heat 1 teaspoon oil add 1/8 teaspoon turmeric powder and 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder. Give a quick mix.

Step 7

Add 3 eggs you can add upto 6 eggs. Give a quick mix toss well. Let oil separate in the gravy. Add the roasted eggs.

Step 8

Cook covered for 3 mins. Oil separates now add 1 tablespoon coriander leaves and switch off.
